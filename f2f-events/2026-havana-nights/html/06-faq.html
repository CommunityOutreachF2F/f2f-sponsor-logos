Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?
Havana Nights / Festive Cocktail — bright colors, dresses, guayabera shirts, linen, fun accessories.
What does my donation support?
Your gift fuels our Client Sponsorship Fund: safe housing, recovery support, transportation, work readiness, and essentials.
Can I sponsor a client directly?
Yes! Popular levels: $180 (one week of housing), $200 (mom & child for a week), $360 (two weeks), $720 (one month), $750 (domestic violence housing), or choose your own amount.
Is my gift tax-deductible?
Foundations to Freedom is a nonprofit; gifts are tax-deductible as allowed by law. A receipt is emailed automatically.
Can I donate if I can’t attend?
Absolutely. Use the Donate button above or the giving options shown. Every gift changes a life.
